ENJOY YOUR STAY

Enjoy your stay like never before.


Royal Marmin Bay Boutique & Art Hotel offers its guests five types of luxury and seductive accommodation options, to meet the needs of even the most demanding travelers. Here is where the hotel's eco-philosophy prevails with energy-autonomous rooms and smart architectural choices offering accommodation in a clean environment.


Luxury and bright rooms (96 in total) fully refurbished and ready to host couples or small families. Take advantage of the fine architecture and delicate design in each of the five types of rooms that will certainly make your stay unforgettable.


Uniquely designed interiors with terracotta and dark blue color, with retro all time classic and minimal style and furnished by the most famous designers with custom made furnishings, your stay in one of the rooms of Royal Marmin Bay Boutique & Art Hotel will ensure you luxury, privacy and relaxation.

STANDARD ROOM MEDITERRANEAN

Enjoy your stay in a comfortable and elegant room. It features a king-size bed with sheets made of natural, Egyptian fabric to relax your senses and give you the most peaceful sleep ever. The bathroom has a shower and all the amenities you need.
